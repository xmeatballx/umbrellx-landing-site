Constellation Overdrive

openStarSystems is a Portland start-up focusing on immersive, interactive multimedia presentations and creative interfaces. The company was founded in 2019 by artists Nolan Thomas and Val Storm– friends who met while students of the PCC Creative Coding program. The duo share a passion for exploration, experimentation, and transcending creative boundaries, producing content sure to wow audiences from any walk of life. www.openstar.systems
